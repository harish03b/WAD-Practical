Famous Landmarks

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. It was a gift from France to the United States and was dedicated on October 28, 1886.

Empire State Building

The Empire State Building is a 102-story Art Deco skyscraper in Midtown Manhattan. It stood as the world's tallest building for nearly 40 years and remains an iconic symbol of New York City.

Central Park

Central Park is an urban park in Manhattan, New York City. It is the most visited urban park in the United States with an estimated 42 million visitors annually.

Times Square

Times Square is a major commercial intersection, tourist destination, entertainment hub, and neighborhood in Midtown Manhattan. It is brightly lit by numerous digital billboards and advertisements.
