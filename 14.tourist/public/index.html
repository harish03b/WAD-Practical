Famous Tourist Places in India

Explore the beauty of incredible India

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in Agra. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

Kerala Backwaters

The Kerala backwaters are a network of brackish lagoons and lakes lying parallel to the Arabian Sea coast. Houseboat cruises in these backwaters are a major tourist attraction, offering beautiful views of the countryside.

Amber Fort, Jaipur

Amber Fort is a magnificent fort located in Amer, Rajasthan. The fort is known for its artistic style elements, with large ramparts and series of gates and cobbled paths. The main attractions are the Sheesh Mahal and Sukh Niwas.

City Palace, Jaipur

The City Palace in Jaipur includes the Chandra Mahal and Mubarak Mahal palaces and other buildings. The palace complex lies in the heart of Jaipur city, and incorporates an impressive and vast array of courtyards, gardens and buildings.
