Basic Tennis Rules

Scoring System

  • Points progress from love (0) to 15, 30, 40, then game
  • If both players reach 40, it's called deuce
  • After deuce, a player must win two consecutive points to win the game
  • First to win 6 games (with a 2-game lead) wins the set
  • Matches are usually best of 3 or 5 sets

Game Rules

  • The ball must land within the boundaries of the court
  • Players get two attempts for a successful serve
  • The ball can only bounce once before being returned
  • Players cannot touch the net or cross over to opponent's side
  • A let is called if the serve hits the net but lands in

Celebrating India's tennis legacy

Ramkumar Ramanathan's Achievements
  • Career-high ATP singles ranking of 111 (achieved in 2018)
  • First Indian to reach an ATP Tour final since 2011 (Newport 2017)
  • Won 5 ITF Futures singles titles and 3 doubles titles
  • Represented India in Davis Cup since 2014
  • Gold medal in men's doubles at 2018 Asian Games (with Rohan Bopanna)
Sania Mirza's Achievements
  • First Indian woman to win a WTA title (2005)
  • 6 Grand Slam doubles titles (3 in women's doubles, 3 in mixed doubles)
  • Former world No. 1 in doubles (achieved in 2015)
  • 4 medals at Asian Games (including 2 golds)
  • Arjuna Award, Padma Shri, and Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna recipient
Leander Paes's Achievements
  • Olympic bronze medal in singles (1996 Atlanta)
  • 18 Grand Slam titles (8 in men's doubles, 10 in mixed doubles)
  • Career Grand Slam in doubles and mixed doubles
  • Most doubles wins in Davis Cup history
  • Padma Bhushan, Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, and Arjuna Award recipient
